About CustomPC.tech

Hi! I’m a young PC builder who makes custom computers for gaming, editing, school, and everything in between. I started CustomPC.tech to help people get the best performance for their budget without confusing specs or prebuilt traps.

I design each PC just for you. No bloatware. No scams. Just clean, optimized power.

Whether you're a content creator needing 64GB of RAM or a Fortnite pro chasing frames, I've got a build for you. Let’s make something awesome together.
