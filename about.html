Our process

1) Consult

We capture your actual tasks: games, apps, resolution, size/noise preferences.

2) Design

We map workloads to parts using reliable benchmarks, then sanity-check PSU, thermals, and bottlenecks.

3) Build & verify

Assembly, cable management, burn-in, and stress tests. We document temps and stability.

4) Deliver & support

Foam-in-case shipping, setup notes, and friendly email support.

FAQ

Do you really design around my workload?

Yes. We pick parts based on what you run (games/apps, resolution, thermals/noise targets) rather than generic tiers.

How do you validate performance?

We translate your tasks to reputable benchmarks and check power/thermals to avoid throttling, then share expected performance ranges.

What’s included in build verification?

Cable management, BIOS setup, burn-in, stress tests, and a brief report on temps and stability.

Can I upgrade later?

We plan headroom and document drop-in upgrade paths for CPU, GPU, RAM, and storage.

Do you ship safely?

Yes—components are secured and foam-in-case is used when appropriate to prevent transit damage.
