Benchmarks & Sourcing

Methodology

We draw from independent sources (TechPowerUp, GN, Puget, UL, SPEC, Blender, Unreal Valley) and normalize by resolution/preset to match your use‑case. We don’t cherry‑pick single runs—outliers are treated carefully.

Parts Sourcing

We track price/stock across reputable retailers and warn about known coil‑whine or cooler‑mount issues. If a part is on watchlist, we provide an alternative with similar performance per dollar.
